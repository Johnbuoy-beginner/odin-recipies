Mexican Stew

Return home

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bonless pork loin cut into 3/4 inch cubes
  • 3 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 2 celery ribs chopped
  • 1 jallapeno pepper seeded and chopped
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • 1-1/2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cummin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (16 ounces) kidney beans rinced and drained
  • 1 can (15 ounces) pinto beans rinced and drained
  • 1 can (14-1/2 ounces) diced tomatos un drained
  • 2 teaspoons minced fresh coriander

Steps

  1. In a dutch oven or large soup kettle over medium high heat, brown meat on all sides in 1 teaspoon of oil; drain, remove meta and keep warm

  2. In the same pan, saute onion and celery, jalapeno and garlic in remaining oil until tender. Stir in water, chili powder, brown sugar, cummin, salt and pepper. Return meat to the pan. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes

  3. Stir in the tomato paste, beans, and tomatos. Return to the boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for another 20 minutes or until meat is tender and the beans are heated through. sprinkle with coriander
