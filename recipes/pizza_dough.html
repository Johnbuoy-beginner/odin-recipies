Pizza Dough

Return Home

Ingredients

  • 2 tsps (7g sachet) dried yeast
  • 1 cup (250ml) luke warm water
  • 2 2/3 cups (400g) plain flour
  • 2 tsps salt
  • 3 tbsps (1/4 cup) olive oil

Steps

  1. Whisk yeast into water in a heatproof Jug leave to stand for five minutes or until frothy

  2. Place flour and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the centre. Pour the yeast mixture in with the oil into the well. using your hands mix together until the dough comes together and is smooth. Turn out onto a lightly floured surface and knead briefly

  3. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl and cover in cling wrap. Set aside in a warm place for 30 minutes to an hour until it has doubled in size

  4. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead gently for 5 muniute until smooth. Divide dough and roll out into pizza bases of the desired size. Sprinkle a little flour onto the bench to stop the dough from sticking. Add your favourite toppings.
