Taco Seasoning

mild

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cummin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprila
  • 1 dash chili powder
  • 1 lb ground meat, chiken, or fish
  • 2/3 cup of water

Steps

  1. Cook or heat up meat or fish

  2. Put all spices in a bowl and mix together

  3. Add mixed onto cooked meat and stir until coated

  4. Add water and stir until mixed through

  5. Simmer on low heat stiring occasionally untill sauce reduces, then serve
